02 — The Problem

The Core Challenge

With the evolution of 5G and 6G networks, security becomes a critical challenge that legacy tools simply cannot address.

01 / STATIC
Static Rules
Tools like Snort rely on fixed rule sets. They cannot adapt to new, evolving attack patterns in real time — leaving your network perpetually exposed to novel threats.
02 / NOISE
Alert Fatigue
Thousands of false positives overwhelm analysts daily — critical threats are buried and missed in the noise, leading to delayed or zero response.
03 / SPEED
Speed Mismatch
Sub-1ms 6G traffic moves faster than any human inspection or signature-matching approach can keep pace with. Manual review is impossible at scale.
04 / BLIND
No Explainability
Analysts cannot understand why something was flagged — no trust, no context, no action path. Black-box systems create paralysis, not protection.
